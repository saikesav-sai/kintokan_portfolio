About

The Diploma in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at IIIT provides students with a strong foundation in computing principles, software development, and hardware systems. This program prepares students for careers in technology and further studies in computer science.

Key Components

  • Programming and Algorithms: C, C++, Python, data structures, and algorithm design.
  • Software Development: Web development, mobile app development, and software engineering practices.
  • Computer Networks & Security: Networking protocols, cybersecurity fundamentals, and cryptography.
  • Database Management Systems: SQL, NoSQL, and cloud databases.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning: Basics of AI, ML models, and deep learning applications.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on coding projects and real-world applications.
  • Internships in software companies, research labs, and startups.
  • Work with cloud computing, AI tools, and cybersecurity frameworks.

Career Opportunities

  • Data Scientist
  • Network Engineer
  • Cybersecurity Analyst
