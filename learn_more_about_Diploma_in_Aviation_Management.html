Diploma in Aviation Management

Comprehensive training in airline operations and aviation business management

About

A diploma in Aviation Management provides students with in-depth knowledge of the aviation industry, including airport operations, airline management, safety regulations, and customer service. This program is designed to prepare students for administrative and managerial roles in the aviation sector.

Key Components

  • Airport and Airline Operations: Understanding the functioning of airports, airlines, and air traffic management.
  • Aviation Safety and Security: Training on international safety regulations, emergency procedures, and security protocols.
  • Airline Marketing and Customer Service: Learning about passenger handling, ticketing, and customer satisfaction in the aviation sector.
  • Logistics and Cargo Management: Handling freight operations, logistics, and international trade regulations.
  • Aviation Law and Regulations: Understanding policies, international aviation laws, and government regulations.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with airport ground operations and airline management.
  • Training on aviation software and reservation systems.
  • Simulated exercises in airport security and emergency response.
  • Internships at airports, airlines, and aviation companies.

  • Airline Operations Executive
  • Air Cargo Coordinator
  • Flight Dispatcher
