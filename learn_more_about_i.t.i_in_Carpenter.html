Diploma in Carpentry - ITI

Comprehensive training in woodworking and carpentry techniques

About

The Diploma in Carpentry at ITI provides students with essential woodworking skills, knowledge of carpentry tools, and construction techniques. This program prepares students for careers in woodworking, furniture making, and construction industries.

Key Components

  • Woodworking Basics: Cutting, shaping, and assembling wood structures.
  • Carpentry Tools: Usage and maintenance of hand and power tools.
  • Furniture Making: Designing and constructing wooden furniture.
  • Construction Carpentry: Framing, roofing, and flooring techniques.
  • Finishing Techniques: Polishing, painting, and varnishing wood surfaces.

Practical Training

  • Workshops on furniture making and joinery.
  • Internships in construction companies and woodworking firms.
  • Hands-on experience with modern and traditional carpentry techniques.

Career Opportunities

  • Carpenter
  • Furniture Designer
  • Construction Worker
  • Woodworking Technician
  • Interior Woodwork Specialist

Further Studies

  • Advanced certifications in woodworking and construction.
  • Specialized training in interior design and furniture craftsmanship.

Summary

The Diploma in Carpentry at ITI equips students with the technical skills and knowledge required to excel in woodworking, furniture making, and construction industries. This program provides a strong foundation for professional growth and career advancement in carpentry.
