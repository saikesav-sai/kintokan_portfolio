Diploma in Interior Designing

Comprehensive training in interior design principles and spatial aesthetics

About

A diploma in Interior Designing provides students with essential knowledge of space planning, aesthetics, color theory, and material selection. This program prepares students for careers in interior design and decoration.

Key Components

  • Design Principles: Fundamentals of space planning, balance, and harmony.
  • Color Theory: Understanding the psychology and application of colors.
  • Material and Textures: Selection of furniture, fabrics, and finishes.
  • CAD and 3D Modeling: Using digital tools for interior design projects.
  • Lighting and Decor: Techniques for enhancing interiors with proper lighting.

Practical Training

  • Internships with architects, interior designers, or decor firms.
  • Live projects and client-based assignments.
  • Workshops on furniture design, home styling, and sustainable interiors.

Career Opportunities

  • Space Planner
  • Furniture Designer
  • Set Designer for Film & TV
  • Home Decor Consultant
