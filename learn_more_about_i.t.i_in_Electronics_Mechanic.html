Diploma in Electronics Mechanic - ITI

Comprehensive training in electronics and electrical maintenance

About

The Diploma in Electronics Mechanic at ITI provides students with practical and theoretical knowledge of electronic circuits, repair, and maintenance. This program prepares students for careers in electronics manufacturing, servicing, and maintenance industries.

Key Components

  • Basic Electronics: Fundamentals of circuits, semiconductors, and components.
  • Electrical Machines: Understanding motors, transformers, and power supply systems.
  • PCB Designing and Soldering: Assembling and troubleshooting printed circuit boards.
  • Microcontroller and PLC: Basics of automation and embedded systems.
  • Repair and Maintenance: Diagnosing and fixing electronic appliances and industrial equipment.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with circuit assembly and troubleshooting.
  • Internships in electronics repair shops, manufacturing units, and automation industries.
  • Exposure to industry-standard testing tools and equipment.

Career Opportunities

  • Electronics Technician
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • PCB Assembly Operator
  • Appliance Repair Specialist
  • Automation Technician

Further Studies

  • Advanced diplomas in Industrial Automation or Embedded Systems.
  • Certifications in PLC programming, microcontroller applications, and electronics design.

Summary

The Diploma in Electronics Mechanic at ITI equips students with hands-on expertise in electronics repair, maintenance, and automation. It provides a strong foundation for technical careers and further education in electronics engineering.
