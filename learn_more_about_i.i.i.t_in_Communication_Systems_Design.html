Diploma in Communication Systems Design - IIIT

Comprehensive training in communication technologies and system design

About

The Diploma in Communication Systems Design at IIIT focuses on modern communication technologies, including wireless systems, signal processing, and networking. This program prepares students for careers in telecommunications, embedded systems, and research.

Key Components

  • Wireless Communication: Fundamentals of 4G/5G networks, satellite communication, and IoT connectivity.
  • Signal Processing: Digital signal processing, modulation techniques, and noise reduction.
  • Network Design: Network protocols, routing algorithms, and cybersecurity principles.
  • Embedded Systems: Microcontrollers, FPGA programming, and real-time communication systems.
  • Communication Standards & Regulations: IEEE standards, spectrum management, and compliance policies.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects in communication system simulations and hardware design.
  • Internships in telecom companies, embedded system firms, and research labs.
  • Exposure to industry-standard tools like MATLAB, NS3, and hardware platforms.
