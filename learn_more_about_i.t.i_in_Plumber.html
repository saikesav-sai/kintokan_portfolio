ITI in Plumber

Comprehensive training in plumbing systems and maintenance

About

The ITI in Plumber course provides students with hands-on training in plumbing systems, pipe fitting, water supply management, and sanitation. This program prepares students for careers in residential, commercial, and industrial plumbing.

Key Components

  • Plumbing Basics: Installation and maintenance of pipes, fittings, and fixtures.
  • Water Supply Systems: Understanding water distribution, storage, and purification.
  • Sanitation & Drainage: Waste disposal, drainage systems, and sewage management.
  • Welding & Pipe Fitting: Techniques for joining pipes and metalwork.
  • Safety & Regulations: Understanding plumbing codes, safety measures, and environmental regulations.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on training in pipe installation, repair, and maintenance.
  • Internships in construction companies, municipalities, and plumbing firms.
  • Workshops on welding, blueprint reading, and plumbing tools.

Career Opportunities

  • Plumber Technician
  • Pipe Fitter
  • Sanitary Inspector
  • Maintenance Supervisor
  • Self-employed Plumber

Further Studies

  • Advanced certification in plumbing and sanitation.
  • Specialized training in HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning).

Summary

The ITI in Plumber course equips students with essential plumbing skills, preparing them for technical roles in the plumbing industry. This program offers a solid foundation for both employment and further specialization in the field.
