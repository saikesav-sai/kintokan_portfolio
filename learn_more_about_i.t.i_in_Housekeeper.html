ITI in Housekeeping

Professional training in housekeeping and hospitality management

About

The ITI in Housekeeping program provides students with essential skills in hospitality, cleaning management, and customer service. It prepares individuals for roles in hotels, resorts, corporate offices, and facility management.

Key Components

  • Cleaning and Maintenance: Techniques for professional cleaning and hygiene maintenance.
  • Hospitality Services: Customer service, front office operations, and guest management.
  • Laundry Operations: Washing, ironing, and handling of different fabric types.
  • Safety and Sanitation: Occupational health, hygiene standards, and waste management.
  • Inventory Management: Handling housekeeping supplies, procurement, and stock maintenance.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience in hotels, hospitals, and corporate facilities.
  • Internships in housekeeping departments of reputed hospitality businesses.
  • Training with modern cleaning equipment and chemicals.

Career Opportunities

  • Housekeeping Supervisor
  • Hotel Room Attendant
  • Facility Manager
  • Laundry Supervisor
  • Hospital Housekeeping Staff

Further Studies

  • Diploma in Hospitality Management.
  • Advanced certifications in hotel management and facility operations.

Summary

The ITI in Housekeeping provides specialized training in cleaning, hospitality services, and facility management. Graduates are well-equipped for roles in the hospitality and service industries, ensuring high standards of cleanliness and customer satisfaction.
