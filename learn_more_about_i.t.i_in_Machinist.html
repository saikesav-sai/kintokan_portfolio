ITI in Machinist

Technical training in precision machining and manufacturing

About

The ITI in Machinist program provides students with hands-on training in machine operation, metal cutting, and manufacturing processes. This course prepares individuals for careers in the engineering and manufacturing industries.

Key Components

  • Machine Operations: Training on lathes, milling machines, and grinders.
  • Precision Measurement: Using micrometers, vernier calipers, and gauges.
  • Manufacturing Processes: Metal cutting, shaping, and finishing techniques.
  • Blueprint Reading: Understanding technical drawings and CAD models.
  • Industrial Safety: Workplace safety, equipment handling, and hazard management.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on training with industrial machinery and tools.
  • Internships in manufacturing plants and machine shops.
  • Exposure to CNC programming and automation technology.

Career Opportunities

  • Machinist
  • CNC Operator
  • Tool and Die Maker
  • Quality Control Inspector
  • Manufacturing Technician

Further Studies

  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering or Production Engineering.
  • Certifications in CNC programming and advanced machining.

Summary

The ITI in Machinist course equips students with the technical skills required for the machining and manufacturing industries. With practical training and industry exposure, graduates can pursue careers in precision engineering and industrial production.
