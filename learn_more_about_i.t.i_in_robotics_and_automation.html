Diploma in Robotics and Automation - ITI

About

The Diploma in Robotics and Automation at ITI provides students with hands-on training in robotic systems, automation technologies, and industrial applications. This program prepares students for careers in manufacturing, automation, and smart technology industries.

Key Components

  • Robotics Fundamentals: Design, control, and programming of robotic systems.
  • Automation Technologies: PLC programming, industrial automation, and IoT applications.
  • Embedded Systems: Microcontrollers, sensors, and real-time operating systems.
  • Mechanical and Electrical Systems: Integration of mechanical design with electronic control systems.
  • AI and Machine Learning: Applications of AI in robotics and automation.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with robotic arms, automation tools, and industrial controllers.
  • Internships in manufacturing plants, automation companies, and research labs.
  • Project-based learning in industrial robotics, AI-driven automation, and smart manufacturing.

Career Opportunities

  • Robotics Technician
  • Automation Engineer
  • Industrial Maintenance Specialist
  • Embedded Systems Developer
  • AI-Driven Automation Specialist

Further Studies

  • Advanced certifications in robotics, PLC programming, and AI in automation.
  • Higher studies in Mechatronics, Industrial Automation, or AI-driven Robotics.

Summary

The Diploma in Robotics and Automation at ITI equips students with technical expertise in automation, robotics, and industrial applications. It provides a strong foundation for careers in manufacturing, AI-driven automation, and smart industries.
