Diploma in Supply Chain Management

Comprehensive training in logistics, procurement, and inventory management

About

A diploma in Supply Chain Management provides students with expertise in logistics, procurement, transportation, and inventory management. It prepares individuals for careers in supply chain operations or further studies in business logistics.

Key Components

  • Logistics and Distribution: Managing the flow of goods, transportation, and warehouse operations.
  • Procurement and Sourcing: Supplier selection, negotiation strategies, and purchasing processes.
  • Inventory Management: Stock control, demand forecasting, and storage optimization.
  • Supply Chain Analytics: Data-driven decision-making and performance optimization.
  • Global Supply Chain: International trade, regulations, and risk management.

Practical Training

  • Internships in logistics firms, manufacturing companies, or retail supply chains.
  • Case studies and simulations to develop strategic planning skills.
  • Hands-on experience with supply chain management software and ERP systems.

  • Procurement Specialist
