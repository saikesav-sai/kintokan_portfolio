Diploma in Computer Science and Biosciences (CSB) - IIIT

Interdisciplinary training in computing and biosciences

About

The Diploma in Computer Science and Biosciences (CSB) at IIIT offers a unique blend of computational techniques and biological sciences. This interdisciplinary program enables students to apply computer science in solving biological and healthcare challenges.

Key Components

  • Computer Science Fundamentals: Programming, data structures, and bioinformatics algorithms.
  • Biological Sciences: Genetics, molecular biology, and systems biology.
  • Bioinformatics: Computational analysis of biological data, genome sequencing, and structural biology.
  • Data Science & AI in Biology: Machine learning applications in drug discovery and personalized medicine.
  • Healthcare Technology: Medical imaging, diagnostics, and biotech innovations.

Practical Training

  • Projects integrating computer science with biosciences research.
  • Internships in biotech companies, healthcare firms, and research labs.
  • Hands-on experience with bioinformatics tools, AI-driven healthcare applications, and laboratory simulations.

Career Opportunities

  • Computational Biologist
  • Software Developer in Healthcare Tech
  • Genomics Data Scientist
  • AI Researcher in Biomedicine

Summary
