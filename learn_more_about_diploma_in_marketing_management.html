Diploma in Marketing Management

About

A diploma in Marketing Management provides students with essential knowledge and skills in market analysis, branding, digital marketing, and business promotion. The program prepares students for careers in marketing, advertising, and sales.

Key Components

  • Market Research: Understanding consumer behavior and market trends.
  • Brand Management: Strategies for building and maintaining a strong brand identity.
  • Digital Marketing: SEO, social media marketing, and content marketing.
  • Advertising and Promotion: Campaign planning, media buying, and public relations.
  • Sales Techniques: Customer relationship management and sales strategies.

Practical Training

  • Internships in marketing agencies, corporate marketing departments, and startups.
  • Case studies and real-world marketing projects.
  • Hands-on experience with digital marketing tools and advertising platforms.

Career Opportunities

  • Marketing Executive
  • Brand Manager
  • Digital Marketing Specialist
  • Sales and Business Development Manager
  • Market Research Analyst
