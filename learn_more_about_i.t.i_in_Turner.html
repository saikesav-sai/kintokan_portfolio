ITI in Turner

Industrial training in machining and turning operations

About

The ITI in Turner program provides specialized training in machining processes, lathe operations, and precision engineering. It prepares students for technical roles in manufacturing and mechanical industries.

Key Components

  • Lathe Machine Operations: Turning, facing, drilling, and threading.
  • Precision Measurement: Use of calipers, micrometers, and gauges.
  • Engineering Drawing: Understanding blueprints and mechanical designs.
  • Machine Maintenance: Lubrication, alignment, and troubleshooting of lathes.
  • Safety and Quality Control: Workplace safety standards and quality inspection.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on training with lathe and milling machines.
  • Workshop practice with cutting tools and measuring instruments.
  • Internships in manufacturing plants and mechanical workshops.

Career Opportunities

  • Lathe Machine Operator
  • Turner/Fitter
  • Machinist
  • Production Supervisor
  • Quality Control Technician

Further Studies

  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering.
  • Advanced certifications in CNC programming and machining technology.

Summary

The ITI in Turner program equips students with essential machining skills and knowledge for careers in the manufacturing industry. It provides a strong foundation for technical growth and further education in mechanical engineering.
