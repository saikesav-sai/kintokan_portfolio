ITI in Sewing Technology

Comprehensive training in sewing, tailoring, and garment making

About

The ITI in Sewing Technology program provides students with fundamental knowledge and hands-on skills in garment construction, tailoring techniques, and textile design. This course prepares students for careers in the apparel industry or self-employment as skilled tailors.

Key Components

  • Basic Sewing Techniques: Stitching, hemming, and pattern making.
  • Garment Construction: Cutting, fitting, and sewing different types of clothing.
  • Textile & Fabric Knowledge: Understanding different fabrics, dyes, and finishes.
  • Machine Operations: Training on industrial sewing machines and embroidery machines.
  • Entrepreneurship Skills: Starting a tailoring business, customer handling, and marketing.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on practice in stitching and garment construction.
  • Internships with fashion designers, boutiques, or textile industries.
  • Exposure to modern sewing techniques and tools.

Career Opportunities

  • Tailor
  • Fashion Designer Assistant
  • Garment Production Supervisor
  • Embroidery Specialist
  • Self-Employed Boutique Owner

Further Studies

  • Advanced certifications in fashion design and textile technology.
  • Diploma or degree in Fashion Technology or Apparel Design.

Summary

The ITI in Sewing Technology program equips students with essential tailoring and garment-making skills, preparing them for careers in the textile industry or entrepreneurship. It provides a strong foundation in both technical and creative aspects of sewing.
