Diploma in Law

About

A diploma in Law provides students with foundational knowledge of legal systems, judicial procedures, and basic legal concepts. This program prepares students for entry-level roles in legal support services or further studies in law.

Key Components

  • Legal Foundations: Overview of legal systems, principles, and jurisprudence.
  • Contract Law: Essentials of agreements, obligations, and enforcement.
  • Criminal Law: Fundamental concepts in crime, punishment, and legal procedures.
  • Civil Law: Understanding disputes, liabilities, and civil court procedures.
  • Human Rights Law: Principles of human rights and international legal frameworks.

Practical Training

  • Internships in law firms, corporate legal departments, and court offices.
  • Case study analysis and mock trials.
  • Legal research and drafting of legal documents.

  • Paralegal
  • Compliance Officer
