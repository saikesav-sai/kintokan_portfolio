ITI in Internet of Things (IoT) Technician

Hands-on training in IoT technology and applications

About

The ITI in Internet of Things (IoT) Technician program provides foundational knowledge and practical skills in IoT technology. This course prepares students for careers in smart technology, automation, and embedded systems.

Key Components

  • IoT Fundamentals: Basics of IoT architecture, protocols, and applications.
  • Embedded Systems: Working with microcontrollers, sensors, and actuators.
  • Networking & Communication: IoT connectivity using Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and LPWAN.
  • Cloud Computing & Data Analytics: Storing and analyzing IoT data on cloud platforms.
  • Cybersecurity & Privacy: Securing IoT devices and networks.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with Raspberry Pi, Arduino, and IoT sensors.
  • IoT project development for smart home, healthcare, and industrial applications.
  • Internships with IoT companies and tech startups.

Career Opportunities

  • IoT Technician
  • Embedded Systems Developer
  • Network & IoT Security Specialist
  • Smart Device Installer
  • Automation Engineer

Further Studies

  • Diploma or Bachelor's degree in IoT, Embedded Systems, or Computer Science.
  • Certifications in IoT security, cloud computing, and automation technologies.

Summary

The ITI in Internet of Things (IoT) Technician program equips students with the technical expertise to work in the growing IoT industry. With practical training and industry exposure, graduates can pursue careers in automation, smart technologies, and networked systems.
