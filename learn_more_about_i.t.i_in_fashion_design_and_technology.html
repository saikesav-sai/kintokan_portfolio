Diploma in Fashion Design and Technology - ITI

About

The Diploma in Fashion Design and Technology at ITI provides students with essential skills in fashion illustration, garment construction, textile technology, and design principles. This program prepares students for careers in the fashion industry or further studies in fashion design.

Key Components

  • Fashion Illustration: Sketching techniques, design concepts, and fashion trends.
  • Textile Technology: Understanding fabrics, dyeing, and printing techniques.
  • Garment Construction: Pattern making, sewing, and tailoring methods.
  • Fashion Marketing: Branding, merchandising, and retail management.
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD): Digital fashion design tools and software.

Practical Training

  • Workshops on fabric selection, draping, and embroidery.
  • Internships in fashion houses, textile industries, and boutiques.
  • Hands-on experience with fashion design software and industry-standard equipment.

Career Opportunities

  • Fashion Designer
  • Textile Designer
  • Garment Technologist
  • Fashion Illustrator
  • Retail Merchandiser
