Diploma in Retail Management

Comprehensive training in retail operations and customer service

About

A diploma in Retail Management equips students with knowledge of sales, merchandising, customer service, and supply chain management. It prepares individuals for careers in the retail industry or further studies in business and marketing.

Key Components

  • Retail Operations: Store management, inventory control, and point-of-sale systems.
  • Customer Service: Enhancing customer experience, handling complaints, and building loyalty.
  • Sales and Marketing: Promotional strategies, sales techniques, and digital marketing in retail.
  • Supply Chain Management: Logistics, procurement, and vendor management.
  • Visual Merchandising: Store layout, product displays, and branding.

Practical Training

  • Internships in retail stores, e-commerce businesses, or shopping malls.
  • Case studies and real-world retail business simulations.
  • Hands-on experience with POS systems, inventory management, and customer service tools.

Career Opportunities

  • Sales Associate
  • Merchandising Executive
  • Supply Chain Coordinator
  • Customer Service Manager
