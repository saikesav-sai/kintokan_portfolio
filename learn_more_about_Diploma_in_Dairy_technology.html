Diploma in Dairy Technology

Comprehensive training in dairy science and technology

About

A diploma in Dairy Technology provides students with the essential knowledge and skills required for careers in dairy production, processing, and quality management. The program offers hands-on training in dairy product development, safety standards, and dairy farm management.

Key Components

  • Dairy Science & Production: Understanding milk composition, dairy breeds, and farm management.
  • Dairy Processing Technology: Training in milk pasteurization, homogenization, and packaging.
  • Dairy Product Development: Learning the production of cheese, butter, yogurt, and ice cream.
  • Quality Control & Safety: Ensuring dairy hygiene, food safety standards, and testing procedures.
  • Dairy Business & Marketing: Strategies for dairy product marketing and supply chain management.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience in dairy processing units and laboratories.
  • Training in quality assurance and dairy product formulation.
  • Internships or projects with dairy farms and industries.
  • Developing business strategies for dairy enterprises.

Career Opportunities

  • Quality Control Officer
  • Production Manager
  • Dairy Farm Supervisor
  • Food Safety Analyst
