Diploma in Automobile Engineering

About

A diploma in Automobile Engineering provides students with fundamental knowledge of vehicle design, manufacturing, maintenance, and repair. The program focuses on automotive technology, mechanical systems, and modern advancements in automobile engineering.

Key Components

  • Automobile Mechanics: Study of vehicle components, engines, and transmission systems.
  • Vehicle Dynamics: Understanding the principles of motion, control, and stability of automobiles.
  • Automobile Electrical Systems: Learning about wiring, ignition systems, battery technology, and electronic controls.
  • Manufacturing Processes: Techniques involved in automobile production and assembly.
  • Environmental & Safety Standards: Emphasizing emission control, safety regulations, and sustainability in the automotive industry.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with vehicle maintenance and repair.
  • Training in diagnostic tools for troubleshooting automotive issues.
  • Workshops on engine overhauling and fuel system calibration.
  • Internships with automobile companies, garages, and service centers.

Career Opportunities

  • Automobile Technician
  • Service Engineer
  • Automobile Designer
  • Quality Control Engineer
  • Workshop Supervisor
