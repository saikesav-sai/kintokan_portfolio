Diploma in Fashion Designing

About

A diploma in Fashion Designing provides students with knowledge of fashion trends, textile science, and garment construction. This program equips students with creative and technical skills to design apparel and accessories.

Key Components

  • Fashion Illustration: Sketching and designing clothing concepts.
  • Textile Science: Understanding fabrics, dyes, and materials.
  • Garment Construction: Sewing, pattern making, and draping techniques.
  • Fashion Marketing: Branding, retail, and fashion business management.
  • Portfolio Development: Creating a professional fashion design portfolio.

Practical Training

  • Internships with fashion designers, boutiques, or textile industries.
  • Participation in fashion shows and exhibitions.
  • Hands-on experience with pattern-making software and sewing tools.

Career Opportunities

  • Fashion Designer
  • Textile Designer
  • Apparel Merchandiser
  • Fashion Illustrator
  • Retail Fashion Buyer
