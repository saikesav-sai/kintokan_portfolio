Diploma in Mechanical Systems Design - IIIT

Comprehensive training in mechanical design and engineering principles

About

The Diploma in Mechanical Systems Design at IIIT focuses on fundamental and advanced concepts in mechanical engineering, system modeling, and product design. This program equips students with practical knowledge in mechanical design and analysis.

Key Components

  • Engineering Mechanics: Fundamentals of forces, motion, and structural analysis.
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD): 3D modeling, simulation, and product visualization.
  • Materials Science: Properties and applications of engineering materials.
  • Manufacturing Processes: Machining, automation, and additive manufacturing.
  • Thermodynamics & Fluid Mechanics: Energy systems, heat transfer, and fluid dynamics.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with CAD software and mechanical simulations.
  • Internships in manufacturing plants, automotive industries, and design firms.
  • Industry projects on real-world mechanical design problems.
