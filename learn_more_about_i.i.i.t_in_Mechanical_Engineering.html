Diploma in Mechanical Engineering - IIIT

Comprehensive training in mechanical systems and engineering principles

About

The Diploma in Mechanical Engineering at IIIT provides students with a strong foundation in mechanical principles, thermodynamics, material science, and design. This program prepares students for careers in manufacturing, automation, and product design.

Key Components

  • Mechanical Design: CAD modeling, engineering drawing, and product design.
  • Thermodynamics & Fluid Mechanics: Heat transfer, energy systems, and fluid behavior.
  • Manufacturing Processes: CNC machining, 3D printing, and industrial production techniques.
  • Automation & Robotics: Control systems, sensors, and robotic integration.
  • Materials Science: Properties of materials, material selection, and strength analysis.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on training with CAD software, 3D printing, and CNC machining.
  • Internships in manufacturing, automotive, and robotics industries.
  • Laboratory experiments in thermodynamics, mechanics, and materials science.

Career Opportunities

  • Manufacturing Specialist
