Diploma in Airport Ground Training

About

A diploma in Airport Ground Training prepares students for essential roles in airport operations, including passenger handling, security procedures, baggage handling, and customer service. The program provides both theoretical knowledge and hands-on training to ensure graduates are well-equipped for ground staff roles.

Key Components

  • Passenger Handling: Training in check-in procedures, boarding processes, and customer assistance.
  • Security & Safety: Understanding airport security regulations, emergency protocols, and risk management.
  • Baggage Handling: Training in baggage screening, handling, and logistics operations.
  • Airline Ticketing & Reservations: Learning about ticketing software, fare calculations, and booking procedures.
  • Airport Operations: Understanding airport infrastructure, air traffic control coordination, and regulatory guidelines.

Practical Training

  • Simulated airport environment training.
  • Emergency response and crisis management exercises.
  • Hands-on experience in customer service and passenger management.
  • Internships or job placements with airlines and airport service providers.
