Diploma in Integrated Product Design - IIIT

Comprehensive training in product innovation, design, and engineering

About

The Diploma in Integrated Product Design at IIIT blends engineering, design thinking, and business strategy to develop innovative products. This interdisciplinary program prepares students to create user-centric solutions for real-world challenges.

Key Components

  • Design Thinking: Problem-solving, user research, and prototyping.
  • Engineering & Manufacturing: Materials, CAD modeling, and 3D printing.
  • Human-Centered Design: UX/UI principles and ergonomics.
  • Business & Entrepreneurship: Market research, branding, and product strategy.
  • Sustainability & Innovation: Eco-friendly design and emerging technologies.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects integrating design and engineering.
  • Internships in product design firms, startups, and R&D labs.
  • Prototyping using CAD tools, 3D printing, and digital fabrication.

Career Opportunities

  • Product Designer
  • UX/UI Designer
  • Industrial Designer
  • Design Engineer
  • Innovation Consultant

Further Studies

  • Advanced degrees in Product Design, Industrial Design, or Human-Centered Design.
  • Certifications in UX/UI, design software, and prototyping technologies.

Summary

The Diploma in Integrated Product Design at IIIT equips students with the skills to create innovative, user-focused products. With a strong foundation in design, engineering, and business strategy, graduates are prepared for dynamic careers in product development and innovation.
