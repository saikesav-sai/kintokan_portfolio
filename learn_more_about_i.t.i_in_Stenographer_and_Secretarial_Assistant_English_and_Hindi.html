ITI in Stenographer and Secretarial Assistant (English & Hindi)

Professional training in stenography, office management, and secretarial skills

About

The ITI course in Stenographer and Secretarial Assistant (English & Hindi) provides students with essential skills in shorthand, typing, office management, and communication. This program prepares individuals for administrative roles in corporate and government sectors.

Key Components

  • Shorthand Writing: Learning stenography in English and Hindi.
  • Typing Skills: Proficiency in computer typing with speed and accuracy.
  • Office Management: Handling administrative tasks, file management, and office procedures.
  • Business Communication: Professional correspondence, email etiquette, and verbal skills.
  • Computer Applications: MS Office, data entry, and record-keeping software.

Practical Training

  • Internships in corporate offices, government departments, and legal firms.
  • Hands-on training in shorthand writing and transcription.
  • Practical sessions on office automation and secretarial software.

Career Opportunities

  • Stenographer
  • Executive Assistant
  • Office Administrator
  • Data Entry Operator
  • Secretary (Corporate/Government)

Further Studies

  • Advanced diploma in Office Management or Secretarial Practices.
  • Certifications in business communication, computer applications, and shorthand.

Summary

The ITI course in Stenographer and Secretarial Assistant (English & Hindi) provides a solid foundation in stenography, office management, and communication. It equips students with essential administrative skills for careers in both public and private sectors.
