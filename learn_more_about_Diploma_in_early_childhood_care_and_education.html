Diploma in Early Childhood Care and Education

Comprehensive training in early childhood development and education techniques

About

A diploma in Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) prepares students to work with young children, focusing on their physical, emotional, social, and cognitive development. The program provides foundational knowledge and hands-on training in child care and pedagogy.

Key Components

  • Child Development: Understanding the stages of early childhood growth and learning.
  • Teaching Methodologies: Effective teaching techniques for young learners.
  • Classroom Management: Strategies for maintaining a positive learning environment.
  • Health and Nutrition: Basic health care and nutrition for children.
  • Special Needs Education: Supporting children with different learning abilities.

Practical Training

  • Internships in preschools, daycare centers, and primary schools.
  • Lesson planning and execution under supervision.
  • Workshops on storytelling, activity-based learning, and play therapy.

Career Opportunities

  • Daycare Center Coordinator
  • Childcare Worker
  • Education Consultant
