Diploma in Air Hostess & Cabin Crew Training

Comprehensive training in aviation hospitality and in-flight service

About

A diploma in Air Hostess & Cabin Crew Training prepares students for a dynamic career in the aviation industry, focusing on in-flight service, passenger safety, communication skills, and airline operations. The program provides a mix of theoretical knowledge and hands-on training to develop professional cabin crew members.

Key Components

  • Passenger Handling: Training in assisting passengers, safety procedures, and emergency management.
  • Hospitality & Communication: Emphasis on excellent customer service, grooming, and etiquette.
  • Airline Operations: Basics of ticketing, airport security, and cabin crew responsibilities.
  • First Aid & Safety: Training in CPR, fire safety, and handling in-flight emergencies.
  • Foreign Language Skills: Optional training in languages to enhance communication with international passengers.

Practical Training

  • Simulated in-flight service training.
  • Emergency drills and first-aid response exercises.
  • Mock interviews and personality development sessions.
  • Internships or job placements with airlines and hospitality firms.

Career Opportunities

  • Air Hostess / Flight Steward
  • Cabin Crew Trainer
  • Ground Staff and Airport Management
  • Customer Service Executive
  • Corporate Hospitality Roles
