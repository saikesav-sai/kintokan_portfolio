ITI in Fitter

Comprehensive training in mechanical fitting and assembly

About

The ITI in Fitter course provides students with practical and theoretical knowledge of mechanical fitting, assembly, and maintenance. This program prepares students for careers in manufacturing, construction, and heavy machinery industries.

Key Components

  • Machine Tools & Operations: Handling and operating lathe, drilling, and grinding machines.
  • Fitting & Assembly: Cutting, shaping, and joining metal components.
  • Welding & Fabrication: Basic welding techniques and sheet metal work.
  • Engineering Drawing: Reading and interpreting technical blueprints.
  • Maintenance & Safety: Repairing machinery, equipment maintenance, and workplace safety.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on training in workshops with industrial tools and equipment.
  • Internships in manufacturing plants, construction sites, and industrial units.
  • Live projects on machinery assembly, welding, and blueprint execution.

Career Opportunities

  • Fitter Technician
  • Welding Technician
  • Machine Operator
  • Maintenance Engineer
  • Quality Control Inspector

Further Studies

  • Advanced certifications in CNC machining, welding, and industrial safety.
  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering or Tool & Die Making.

Summary

The ITI in Fitter course equips students with essential skills in mechanical fitting, welding, and machine operations. This program provides a strong foundation for careers in the manufacturing and engineering sectors.
