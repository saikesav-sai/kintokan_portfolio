Diploma in Surveyor - ITI

Comprehensive training in land surveying and mapping

About

The Diploma in Surveyor at ITI provides students with essential skills in land surveying, mapping, and geospatial technologies. This program prepares students for careers in construction, urban planning, and infrastructure development.

Key Components

  • Surveying Techniques: Land measurement, topographic surveys, and leveling.
  • Geospatial Technology: GIS, GPS, and remote sensing applications.
  • Construction Surveying: Building layout planning and site surveying.
  • Legal Aspects: Land laws, property boundaries, and documentation.
  • Data Interpretation: Analyzing survey data and creating detailed maps.

Practical Training

  • Fieldwork in topographic and cadastral surveys.
  • Hands-on training with total stations, theodolites, and GPS devices.
  • Internships with construction firms, government agencies, and surveying companies.
