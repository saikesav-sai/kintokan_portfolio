Diploma in Mechanic (Motor Vehicle) - ITI

Comprehensive training in automobile maintenance and repair

About

The Diploma in Mechanic (Motor Vehicle) at ITI provides hands-on training in vehicle maintenance, repair, and diagnostics. This program equips students with technical expertise in automotive mechanics, preparing them for careers in the automobile industry.

Key Components

  • Automobile Engineering Basics: Fundamentals of vehicle design and mechanics.
  • Engine Overhauling: Repair and maintenance of petrol and diesel engines.
  • Vehicle Electrical Systems: Understanding wiring, batteries, and ignition systems.
  • Transmission & Brake Systems: Hands-on training in gear systems, clutches, and braking mechanisms.
  • Workshop Practice: Use of tools, safety protocols, and troubleshooting techniques.

Practical Training

  • Internships in automobile workshops, service centers, and manufacturing units.
  • Hands-on training with real vehicle engines and diagnostic tools.
  • Case studies on common vehicle issues and their solutions.

Career Opportunities

  • Automobile Mechanic
  • Vehicle Service Technician
  • Auto Electrician
  • Workshop Supervisor
  • Entrepreneur (Automobile Repair & Service)

Further Studies

  • Advanced diploma in Automobile Engineering.
  • Certifications in vehicle diagnostics and electric vehicle technology.

Summary

The Diploma in Mechanic (Motor Vehicle) at ITI provides essential skills in automobile maintenance, diagnostics, and repair. This program prepares students for careers in the automotive industry or further specialization in vehicle engineering.
