Diploma in Computer Science and Design (CSD) - IIIT

Interdisciplinary training in computing and design

About

The Diploma in Computer Science and Design (CSD) at IIIT blends computing expertise with design principles. This interdisciplinary program prepares students to create innovative digital solutions with a focus on user experience, aesthetics, and functionality.

Key Components

  • Computer Science Fundamentals: Programming, data structures, and algorithms.
  • User Experience (UX) & Interface Design: Human-centered design, usability testing, and wireframing.
  • Graphics & Multimedia: Computer graphics, animation, and digital media tools.
  • Web & Mobile Development: Frontend and backend development for digital products.
  • Design Thinking: Creative problem-solving methodologies and prototyping.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects integrating computer science and design concepts.
  • Internships in tech firms, design studios, and UI/UX research teams.
  • Experience with design software, web frameworks, and prototyping tools.

Career Opportunities

  • UI/UX Designer
  • Software Developer
  • Game Designer
  • Multimedia Specialist
  • Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) Expert

Further Studies

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Interaction Design, or Multimedia Arts.
  • Advanced certifications in UI/UX, game design, and human-centered computing.

Summary

The Diploma in Computer Science and Design (CSD) at IIIT offers a balanced mix of computing and design knowledge. This foundation prepares students for careers in UI/UX design, game development, and interactive media, shaping the future of digital experiences.
