Diploma in Dressmaking - ITI

Comprehensive training in garment design and dressmaking techniques

About

The Diploma in Dressmaking at ITI provides students with the essential skills in garment construction, tailoring, and fashion design. This program prepares students for careers in the fashion industry or entrepreneurial ventures in dressmaking.

Key Components

  • Garment Construction: Cutting, stitching, and finishing techniques.
  • Fashion Design: Sketching, fabric selection, and trend analysis.
  • Pattern Making: Drafting and draping methods for various clothing styles.
  • Textile Science: Understanding different fabrics, dyes, and materials.
  • Entrepreneurship: Setting up a dressmaking business and customer relations.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with sewing machines, embroidery, and garment finishing.
  • Internships in fashion houses, tailoring units, or self-employment projects.
  • Designing and creating custom clothing items as part of coursework.

Career Opportunities

  • Dressmaker / Tailor
  • Fashion Designer
  • Pattern Maker
  • Textile Consultant
  • Entrepreneur (Boutique Owner)

Further Studies

  • Advanced diploma in Fashion Design or Textile Technology.
  • Certifications in embroidery, fashion merchandising, or apparel production.

Summary

The Diploma in Dressmaking at ITI provides students with hands-on training in garment construction, fashion design, and tailoring skills. This program opens doors to careers in the fashion industry and entrepreneurial ventures in dressmaking and textile arts.
