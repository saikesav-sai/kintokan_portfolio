Diploma in Information Technology (IT) and Electronics System Maintenance - ITI

Comprehensive training in IT and electronics system maintenance

About

The Diploma in Information Technology (IT) and Electronics System Maintenance at ITI provides students with technical skills in computing, networking, and electronics. This program prepares students for careers in IT support, hardware maintenance, and system administration.

Key Components

  • Computer Hardware & Networking: Fundamentals of computer systems, networking, and troubleshooting.
  • Software Installation & Maintenance: Operating systems, software deployment, and IT support.
  • Electronics System Maintenance: Repair and maintenance of electronic circuits and devices.
  • Embedded Systems & IoT: Basics of microcontrollers, IoT applications, and automation.
  • Cybersecurity & Data Protection: Network security, data encryption, and system protection techniques.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on training with computer hardware, networking equipment, and diagnostic tools.
  • Internships in IT companies, service centers, and electronic repair firms.
  • Real-world troubleshooting of IT systems and electronics maintenance.

Career Opportunities

  • IT Support Technician
  • Network Administrator
  • Electronics Maintenance Engineer
  • System Administrator
  • Embedded Systems Technician
