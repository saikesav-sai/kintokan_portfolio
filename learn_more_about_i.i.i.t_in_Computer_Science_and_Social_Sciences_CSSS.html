Diploma in Computer Science and Social Sciences (CSSS) - IIIT

Interdisciplinary training in computing and social sciences

About

The Diploma in Computer Science and Social Sciences (CSSS) at IIIT provides a unique blend of technical and social science education. This interdisciplinary program enables students to understand the impact of technology on society while developing computational skills.

Key Components

  • Computer Science Fundamentals: Programming, data structures, and algorithms.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning: Basics of AI, ML models, and ethical AI considerations.
  • Social Sciences: Sociology, psychology, and the impact of technology on human behavior.
  • Data Analytics: Statistical analysis, data visualization, and real-world applications.
  • Ethical and Legal Aspects: Cybersecurity, digital privacy, and tech regulations.

Practical Training

  • Projects integrating computer science with social impact studies.
  • Internships in tech companies, NGOs, and research institutes.
  • Hands-on experience with programming languages, AI tools, and data analysis software.

  • AI Ethics Researcher

Summary
