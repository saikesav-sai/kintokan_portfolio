ITI in Tool & Die Maker

About

The ITI in Tool & Die Maker program provides students with essential skills in tool designing, die-making, and precision machining. This vocational training prepares students for careers in manufacturing, engineering, and industrial production.

Key Components

  • Tool Design: Fundamentals of designing cutting tools, jigs, and fixtures.
  • Die Making: Techniques in sheet metal die design, mold making, and press tools.
  • Precision Machining: Hands-on training with lathe, milling, and CNC machines.
  • Material Science: Understanding metals, alloys, and heat treatment processes.
  • Industrial Safety & Quality Control: Best practices for workplace safety and maintaining quality standards.

Practical Training

  • Workshop training with industry-standard tools and machines.
  • Internships in manufacturing units, tool rooms, and engineering firms.
  • Live projects focusing on tool design and die-making applications.

Career Opportunities

  • Tool & Die Maker
  • CNC Machinist
  • Manufacturing Technician
  • Quality Control Inspector
  • Production Supervisor

Further Studies

  • Diploma or Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering or Manufacturing Technology.
  • Certifications in CNC programming, CAD/CAM, and industrial automation.

Summary

The ITI in Tool & Die Maker program equips students with specialized skills in precision machining and tool designing, opening doors to careers in the manufacturing and engineering sectors. This vocational training provides a strong foundation for professional growth and technical expertise.
