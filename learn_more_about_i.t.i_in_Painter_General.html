Diploma in Painter (General) - ITI

Comprehensive training in painting techniques and surface preparation

About

The Diploma in Painter (General) at ITI provides students with hands-on training in painting techniques, surface preparation, and color mixing. This program prepares individuals for professional painting roles in construction, interior design, and artistic projects.

Key Components

  • Surface Preparation: Cleaning, sanding, and priming different surfaces.
  • Painting Techniques: Brush, roller, and spray painting methods.
  • Color Mixing & Matching: Understanding color theory and paint composition.
  • Safety & Tools Handling: Proper use of painting tools and adherence to safety guidelines.
  • Decorative Painting: Texturing, stenciling, and artistic finishes.

Practical Training

  • On-site training in residential and commercial painting projects.
  • Hands-on experience with different painting tools and techniques.
  • Workshops on decorative and artistic painting methods.

Career Opportunities

  • Professional Painter
  • Interior Decorator
  • Automotive Painter
  • Construction Site Painter
  • Freelance Artist

Further Studies

  • Advanced certifications in industrial painting and decorative arts.
  • Diploma in Interior Design or Fine Arts.

Summary

The Diploma in Painter (General) at ITI provides a solid foundation in professional painting skills, preparing students for careers in residential, commercial, and artistic painting fields. It offers practical expertise in surface preparation, color application, and decorative finishes.
