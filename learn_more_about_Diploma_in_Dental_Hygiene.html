Diploma in Dental Hygiene

Comprehensive training in oral health and dental care

About

A diploma in Dental Hygiene equips students with the knowledge and practical skills required to assist in oral health care. The program provides hands-on training in dental procedures, patient care, and preventive dentistry.

Key Components

  • Oral Health & Hygiene: Understanding dental hygiene practices and preventive care.
  • Dental Anatomy & Physiology: Learning the structure and function of teeth and gums.
  • Periodontal Care: Training in gum disease prevention and treatment.
  • Radiology & Imaging: Introduction to dental X-rays and imaging techniques.
  • Patient Care & Communication: Developing skills in patient interaction and education.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience in dental clinics.
  • Training in sterilization and infection control procedures.
  • Internships with dental professionals.
  • Patient assessment and oral hygiene instruction.

  • Oral Health Educator
