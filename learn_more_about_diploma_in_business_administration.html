Diploma in Business Administration

About

A diploma in Business Administration equips students with the fundamental knowledge and skills required for careers in business management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. The program provides practical training in business operations, leadership, and decision-making.

Key Components

  • Business Management: Understanding the principles of management, leadership, and organizational behavior.
  • Marketing & Sales: Learning the fundamentals of marketing strategies, sales techniques, and brand management.
  • Financial Accounting: Introduction to financial statements, budgeting, and financial planning.
  • Human Resource Management: Training in recruitment, employee relations, and workplace management.
  • Entrepreneurship & Business Strategy: Developing business plans and understanding market trends.

Practical Training

  • Case studies and real-world business scenarios.
  • Training in business software like Microsoft Office, ERP systems, and CRM tools.
  • Internships or projects with business organizations.
  • Developing business plans and strategies.
