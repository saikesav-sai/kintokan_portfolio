About

The Diploma in Smart Manufacturing at IIIT provides students with expertise in modern manufacturing technologies, automation, and Industry 4.0 concepts. This interdisciplinary program prepares students for careers in advanced manufacturing and industrial automation.

Key Components

  • Industry 4.0 Technologies: IoT, AI, and data-driven manufacturing.
  • Automation & Robotics: Integration of robotics in manufacturing processes.
  • Cyber-Physical Systems: Digital twins, smart sensors, and connected systems.
  • Supply Chain & Logistics: Smart inventory management and real-time tracking.
  • Sustainability & Green Manufacturing: Energy-efficient production and waste reduction.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects using smart manufacturing tools and technologies.
  • Internships in industrial automation, automotive, and electronics industries.
  • Experience with industrial IoT platforms, robotics, and predictive maintenance.
