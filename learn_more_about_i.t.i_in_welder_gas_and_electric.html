ITI in Welder (Gas and Electric)

About

The ITI in Welder (Gas and Electric) program provides students with essential skills in welding technology. It covers various welding processes, safety measures, and practical applications in industries.

Key Components

  • Gas Welding: Fundamentals of oxy-acetylene welding and cutting.
  • Electric Welding: Arc welding, MIG welding, and TIG welding techniques.
  • Safety and Compliance: Workplace safety measures, fire prevention, and protective equipment usage.
  • Blueprint Reading: Understanding welding symbols, drawings, and measurements.
  • Metal Fabrication: Cutting, shaping, and assembling metal structures.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience in welding workshops and fabrication units.
  • Internships in manufacturing and construction industries.
  • Real-world projects in metal joining and structure fabrication.

Career Opportunities

  • Welder (Gas & Electric)
  • Fabrication Technician
  • Pipeline Welder
  • Structural Welder
  • Welding Inspector

Further Studies

  • Advanced certifications in welding technologies.
  • Diploma in Mechanical or Fabrication Engineering.
  • Specialized courses in robotic welding and CNC machining.

Summary

The ITI in Welder (Gas and Electric) program equips students with essential welding skills, preparing them for careers in manufacturing, construction, and industrial sectors. It provides a strong foundation for professional growth in the welding industry.
