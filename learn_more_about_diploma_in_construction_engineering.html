Diploma in Construction Engineering

About

A diploma in Construction Engineering equips students with the technical knowledge and practical skills required for careers in civil engineering, construction management, and infrastructure development. The program provides hands-on training in construction techniques, materials, and project management.

Key Components

  • Structural Engineering: Understanding the principles of building structures and materials.
  • Construction Management: Learning project planning, scheduling, and cost estimation.
  • Surveying & Site Analysis: Training in land surveying techniques and site preparation.
  • Building Materials & Technology: Introduction to modern construction materials and sustainable practices.
  • Health & Safety Regulations: Understanding safety protocols and legal requirements in construction.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with construction projects and site visits.
  • Training in software like AutoCAD, Revit, and Primavera.
  • Internships or projects with construction firms and engineering companies.
  • Developing construction plans and project reports.

Career Opportunities

  • Site Supervisor
  • Structural Engineer
  • Project Manager
  • Quantity Surveyor
