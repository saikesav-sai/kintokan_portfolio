Diploma in Graphic Designing

Comprehensive training in visual communication and digital design

About

A diploma in Graphic Designing provides students with essential skills in visual communication, digital artistry, and design principles. The program covers key aspects of graphic design, including branding, typography, and digital illustration.

Key Components

  • Design Principles: Fundamentals of composition, color theory, and visual hierarchy.
  • Typography: Understanding fonts, typefaces, and their impact on design.
  • Digital Illustration: Creating vector graphics and digital artwork.
  • Branding and Identity: Designing logos, business cards, and brand guidelines.
  • UI/UX Design: Basics of user interface and user experience design.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
  • Live client projects and portfolio development.
  • Workshops on digital marketing and social media graphics.

  • UI/UX Designer
  • Brand Identity Designer
