Diploma in Artificial Intelligence - IIIT

About

The Diploma in Artificial Intelligence at IIIT provides students with in-depth knowledge of AI technologies, machine learning, and data-driven decision-making. This program equips students with skills required to develop intelligent systems and solve real-world problems using AI.

Key Components

  • Fundamentals of AI: Core concepts, history, and AI applications.
  • Machine Learning: Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning techniques.
  • Deep Learning: Neural networks, CNNs, RNNs, and transformer models.
  • Natural Language Processing: Text analytics, chatbots, and language models.
  • AI Ethics and Bias: Responsible AI, fairness, and transparency in AI models.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with AI frameworks like TensorFlow and PyTorch.
  • Real-world projects in healthcare, finance, and automation.
  • Internships in AI research labs and tech companies.
