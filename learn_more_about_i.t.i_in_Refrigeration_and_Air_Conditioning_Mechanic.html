ITI in Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Mechanic

Comprehensive training in refrigeration and air conditioning systems

About

The ITI in Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Mechanic course provides students with in-depth knowledge of refrigeration systems, air conditioning technology, and maintenance techniques. This program prepares students for technical roles in HVAC industries.

Key Components

  • Refrigeration Technology: Basics of refrigeration cycles, components, and cooling systems.
  • Air Conditioning Systems: Installation, repair, and maintenance of air conditioning units.
  • Electrical and Electronic Controls: Study of electrical circuits, sensors, and control systems.
  • Mechanical Maintenance: Troubleshooting, servicing, and preventive maintenance techniques.
  • Environmental and Safety Standards: Knowledge of refrigerants, eco-friendly practices, and safety regulations.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with refrigeration and AC equipment.
  • Internships in HVAC companies, manufacturing units, and repair workshops.
  • Practical exposure to diagnostic tools, gas charging, and system testing.

Career Opportunities

  • Refrigeration Mechanic
  • Air Conditioning Technician
  • HVAC Technician
  • Maintenance Supervisor
  • Service Engineer

Further Studies

  • Advanced certification in HVAC and refrigeration technology.
  • Diploma in Mechanical Engineering or related fields.

Summary

The ITI in Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Mechanic course provides students with essential skills in refrigeration and AC systems, preparing them for careers in the HVAC industry. This program offers practical exposure and industry-relevant training for technical professionals.
