Diploma in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics (CSAM) - IIIT

Integrating computational and mathematical sciences

About

The Diploma in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics (CSAM) at IIIT offers a rigorous curriculum that bridges computing with mathematical foundations. This interdisciplinary program equips students with analytical and computational problem-solving skills.

Key Components

  • Programming and Algorithms: Advanced data structures, algorithm design, and optimization.
  • Mathematical Foundations: Linear algebra, calculus, and discrete mathematics.
  • Computational Methods: Numerical computing, scientific computing, and simulation techniques.
  • Machine Learning and AI: Mathematical modeling in AI, deep learning, and probabilistic models.
  • Data Science and Cryptography: Statistical analysis, encryption methods, and secure computing.

Practical Training

  • Research projects integrating mathematics with computer science applications.
  • Internships in tech firms, financial institutions, and research labs.
  • Hands-on experience with programming, AI frameworks, and mathematical modeling tools.
