B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering + M.Tech in AI and Robotics - IIIT

About

The integrated B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering and M.Tech in AI and Robotics at IIIT provides students with a strong foundation in mechanical systems while equipping them with advanced knowledge in artificial intelligence and robotics. This program prepares graduates for the evolving technological landscape.

Key Components

  • Mechanical Engineering: Fundamentals of thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and materials science.
  • Robotics and Automation: Robot kinematics, control systems, and industrial automation.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Machine learning, neural networks, and AI applications in robotics.
  • Embedded Systems: Microcontrollers, sensors, and real-time processing.
  • Human-Robot Interaction: Cognitive robotics and AI-driven automation.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with robotic arms, automation tools, and AI-driven simulations.
  • Internships in manufacturing, AI research labs, and robotics firms.
  • Capstone projects integrating mechanical engineering and AI for real-world applications.

Career Opportunities

  • Robotics Engineer
  • AI Researcher
  • Automation Specialist
  • Mechanical Design Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Developer

Further Studies

  • Ph.D. in Robotics, AI, or Mechanical Engineering.
  • Specialized certifications in deep learning, industrial automation, and control systems.

Summary

The integrated B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering and M.Tech in AI and Robotics at IIIT blends mechanical principles with advanced AI techniques. This interdisciplinary approach prepares students for innovation in robotics, automation, and AI-driven industries.
