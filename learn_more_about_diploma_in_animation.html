Diploma in Animation

About

A diploma in Animation equips students with the technical skills and creative knowledge required for careers in animation, visual effects, and digital media. This program provides hands-on training in animation software, storytelling, and design principles.

Key Components

  • 2D & 3D Animation: Understanding the fundamentals of character animation, movement, and design.
  • Storyboarding & Concept Art: Learning the process of visual storytelling and developing storyboards.
  • Digital Graphics & Design: Training in Photoshop, Illustrator, and other design software.
  • Visual Effects (VFX): Introduction to special effects, compositing, and motion graphics.
  • Character Rigging & Modeling: Creating and animating 3D characters and environments.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects in animation and digital media.
  • Training in industry-standard software like Maya, Blender, and After Effects.
  • Internships or projects with animation studios and media companies.
  • Portfolio development to showcase creative work.

Career Opportunities

  • Animator
  • Graphic Designer
  • Storyboard Artist
  • VFX Artist
  • Game Designer
