Diploma in Mechanic Diesel - ITI

About

The Diploma in Mechanic Diesel at ITI provides specialized training in diesel engine technology, repair, and maintenance. This program equips students with the skills needed to work in the automotive, industrial, and agricultural sectors.

Key Components

  • Diesel Engine Fundamentals: Working principles, components, and troubleshooting.
  • Fuel Systems: Injection systems, fuel pumps, and efficiency optimization.
  • Engine Overhauling: Dismantling, assembling, and testing of diesel engines.
  • Electrical & Electronic Systems: Battery management, ignition systems, and diagnostics.
  • Workshop Practice: Hands-on experience with tools, safety measures, and engine diagnostics.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on training in workshops with real diesel engines.
  • Internships in automobile service centers, manufacturing units, and transportation companies.
  • Practical exposure to engine diagnostics, maintenance, and repair techniques.

Career Opportunities

  • Diesel Mechanic
  • Automobile Technician
  • Service Engineer
  • Workshop Supervisor
  • Self-Employed Garage Owner

Further Studies

  • Advanced diploma in Automotive Engineering or Mechanical Engineering.
  • Certifications in engine diagnostics, fuel system management, and automobile technology.

Summary

The Diploma in Mechanic Diesel at ITI provides in-depth knowledge and practical skills to work in diesel engine technology. Graduates can pursue careers in automotive repair, industrial maintenance, and transportation, with opportunities for further specialization in the field.
