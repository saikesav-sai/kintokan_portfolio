Diploma in Draughtsman (Mechanical) - ITI

Comprehensive training in mechanical drafting and design

About

The Diploma in Draughtsman (Mechanical) at ITI provides students with specialized skills in technical drawing, CAD software, and mechanical design principles. This program prepares students for careers in manufacturing, engineering, and construction sectors.

Key Components

  • Technical Drawing: Engineering graphics, blueprint reading, and drafting standards.
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD): Hands-on experience with CAD software like AutoCAD and SolidWorks.
  • Mechanical Engineering Basics: Principles of mechanics, materials, and machine design.
  • Manufacturing Processes: Metal fabrication, machining, and assembly techniques.
  • Quality Control & Safety: Inspection techniques, tolerances, and workplace safety regulations.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects in mechanical drafting and CAD modeling.
  • Internships in manufacturing plants, engineering firms, and design consultancies.
  • Exposure to real-world industrial applications and technical problem-solving.

Career Opportunities

  • Mechanical Draughtsman
  • CAD Technician
  • Design Engineer Assistant
  • Quality Control Inspector
  • Manufacturing Supervisor

Further Studies

  • Advanced certifications in CAD, 3D modeling, and mechanical design.
  • Diploma or Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering or Industrial Design.

Summary

The Diploma in Draughtsman (Mechanical) at ITI equips students with essential drafting and design skills, preparing them for careers in engineering, manufacturing, and construction. It provides a strong foundation for professional growth in the mechanical industry.
