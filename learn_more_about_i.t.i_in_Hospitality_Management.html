About

The Diploma in Hospitality Management at ITI equips students with essential skills in customer service, hotel operations, event planning, and tourism management. This program prepares students for dynamic roles in the hospitality and service industries.

Key Components

  • Hotel & Restaurant Management: Principles of running hotels, restaurants, and resorts.
  • Customer Service Excellence: Enhancing guest experiences and handling client interactions.
  • Event Planning & Tourism: Organizing events, conferences, and managing tourism services.
  • Food & Beverage Management: Basics of culinary operations, menu planning, and catering services.
  • Hospitality Marketing: Branding, promotions, and digital marketing strategies for hospitality businesses.

Practical Training

  • Internships in hotels, resorts, restaurants, and event management firms.
  • Hands-on experience with hospitality software and customer service tools.
  • Live projects in event coordination and tourism management.
