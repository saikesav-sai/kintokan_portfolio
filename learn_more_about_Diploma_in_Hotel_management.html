Diploma in Hotel Management

Comprehensive training in hospitality, customer service, and hotel operations

About

A diploma in Hotel Management prepares students for careers in the hospitality industry, focusing on hotel operations, food and beverage services, customer relations, and event management.

Key Components

  • Hotel Operations: Understanding front office, housekeeping, and administrative tasks.
  • Food and Beverage Management: Restaurant operations, catering services, and menu planning.
  • Customer Service: Effective communication and guest relationship management.
  • Event Planning: Organizing corporate events, weddings, and hospitality functions.
  • Hospitality Marketing: Strategies for branding, promotions, and customer acquisition.

Practical Training

  • Internships in hotels, resorts, and restaurants.
  • Hands-on experience in housekeeping, front office, and kitchen operations.
  • Workshops on food presentation, customer service, and event coordination.
