Diploma in Physics - IIIT

Comprehensive training in theoretical and applied physics

About

The Diploma in Physics at IIIT provides a strong foundation in classical and modern physics, preparing students for careers in research, technology, and applied sciences. The program covers fundamental principles along with hands-on experimental techniques.

Key Components

  • Classical Mechanics: Newtonian mechanics, kinematics, and dynamics.
  • Quantum Mechanics: Fundamentals of quantum theory and its applications.
  • Electromagnetism: Maxwell's equations, circuits, and electromagnetic waves.
  • Thermodynamics & Statistical Mechanics: Heat transfer, entropy, and statistical methods.
  • Experimental Physics: Laboratory techniques, data analysis, and scientific instrumentation.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experiments in state-of-the-art physics laboratories.
  • Internships in research institutes, tech companies, and government labs.
  • Computational simulations and modeling in physics.
