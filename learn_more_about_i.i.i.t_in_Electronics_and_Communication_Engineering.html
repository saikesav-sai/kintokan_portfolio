Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering - IIIT

Comprehensive training in electronics, communication, and embedded systems

About

The Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering at IIIT provides a strong foundation in electronic systems, communication networks, and embedded technologies. This program equips students with practical skills and theoretical knowledge for careers in the electronics industry.

Key Components

  • Electronic Circuits: Design and analysis of analog and digital circuits.
  • Communication Systems: Wireless communication, signal processing, and network protocols.
  • Microcontrollers & Embedded Systems: Programming and interfacing microcontrollers for real-world applications.
  • VLSI & Semiconductor Devices: Basics of chip design, fabrication, and semiconductor technology.
  • Internet of Things (IoT): Applications of IoT in smart devices and automation.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with circuit design and simulation tools.
  • Internships in electronics, telecommunications, and semiconductor industries.
  • Projects in embedded systems, robotics, and IoT applications.
