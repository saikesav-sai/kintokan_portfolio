Diploma in Carpentry

About

A diploma in Carpentry equips students with the fundamental knowledge and hands-on skills required for careers in woodworking, construction, and furniture making. The program provides practical training in carpentry tools, techniques, and project management.

Key Components

  • Woodworking Techniques: Understanding cutting, shaping, and joining wood materials.
  • Blueprint Reading & Design: Learning to interpret construction drawings and plans.
  • Construction & Framing: Training in building frameworks, walls, and flooring structures.
  • Furniture Making: Developing skills in crafting custom furniture and cabinetry.
  • Safety & Tool Handling: Proper use of hand and power tools while following safety protocols.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects in woodworking and carpentry.
  • Training in industry-standard tools and equipment.
  • Internships or projects with construction and woodworking companies.
  • Portfolio development showcasing completed projects.

Career Opportunities

  • Carpenter
  • Furniture Maker
  • Construction Worker
  • Woodworking Technician
  • Cabinet Maker
