Diploma in Draughtsman (Civil) - ITI

Comprehensive training in civil drafting and design

About

The Diploma in Draughtsman (Civil) at ITI provides specialized training in civil engineering drafting, construction planning, and technical drawing. This program prepares students for careers in the construction and infrastructure industry.

Key Components

  • Technical Drawing: Fundamentals of engineering drawing and blueprint reading.
  • Building Planning: Principles of construction design and structural planning.
  • Surveying: Land surveying techniques and mapping.
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD): Training in AutoCAD and other drafting software.
  • Construction Materials: Understanding different building materials and their applications.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience with drafting tools and CAD software.
  • Internships in construction companies, architecture firms, and government projects.
  • Field training in surveying and site analysis.

Career Opportunities

  • Civil Draughtsman
  • CAD Technician
  • Construction Supervisor
  • Surveying Assistant
  • Structural Detailer

Further Studies

  • Advanced certifications in CAD, structural design, and civil engineering.
  • Diploma or Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering or Architecture.

Summary

The Diploma in Draughtsman (Civil) at ITI equips students with essential skills in drafting, design, and construction planning. It provides a strong foundation for careers in civil engineering and infrastructure development.
