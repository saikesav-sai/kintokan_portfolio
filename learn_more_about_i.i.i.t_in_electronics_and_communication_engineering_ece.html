Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) - IIIT

About

The Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) at IIIT provides a strong foundation in electronic circuits, communication systems, and embedded technologies. This program equips students with the technical skills needed for careers in telecommunications, embedded systems, and signal processing.

Key Components

  • Electronic Circuits: Fundamentals of circuit design, semiconductor devices, and PCB design.
  • Communication Systems: Analog and digital communication, wireless networks, and IoT.
  • Signal Processing: Digital signal processing (DSP) techniques and applications.
  • Embedded Systems: Microcontrollers, real-time operating systems (RTOS), and IoT applications.
  • VLSI & Semiconductor Technology: Basics of Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) and chip design.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on projects in circuit design, embedded systems, and wireless communication.
  • Internships in semiconductor industries, telecommunications, and IoT startups.
  • Laboratory work using simulation tools, microcontrollers, and FPGA programming.

Career Opportunities

  • Electronics Engineer
  • Embedded Systems Developer
  • Communication Engineer
  • IoT Specialist
  • VLSI Design Engineer
