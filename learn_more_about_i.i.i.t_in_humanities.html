Diploma in Humanities - IIIT

About

The Diploma in Humanities at IIIT provides a comprehensive understanding of human culture, society, and critical thinking. This interdisciplinary program enables students to analyze social issues, ethics, and historical perspectives while enhancing communication and research skills.

Key Components

  • Philosophy & Ethics: Understanding moral theories, ethics in technology, and philosophical thought.
  • History & Culture: Exploration of historical events, cultural studies, and their impact on society.
  • Sociology & Psychology: Study of human behavior, social structures, and psychological theories.
  • Communication & Media Studies: Journalism, digital media, and the role of communication in society.
  • Research & Critical Thinking: Analytical reasoning, qualitative and quantitative research methodologies.

Practical Training

  • Research projects on contemporary social issues.
  • Internships in media organizations, NGOs, and research institutions.
  • Workshops on public speaking, writing, and cultural analysis.
