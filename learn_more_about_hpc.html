About HPC

The HPC (Humanities, Psychology, and Civics) stream is ideal for students interested in social sciences, law, psychology, public administration, and related fields. This stream focuses on subjects like humanities, psychology, and civics, equipping students with critical thinking, analytical, and communication skills.

What is HPC?

The HPC stream refers to the combination of Humanities, Psychology, and Civics/Political Science. It provides an in-depth understanding of human behavior, governance, and societal structures. Students can study subjects like History, Geography, Sociology, Psychology, and Political Science, which are suited for careers in social services, law, teaching, and public administration.

Why Choose HPC?

The HPC stream is ideal for students who:

  • Have an interest in understanding human behavior, politics, and society.
  • Wish to pursue careers in psychology, law, teaching, or public administration.
  • Want to contribute to social change through roles in social work, policy, or government.
  • Have strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.

Competitive Exams after Completing HPC

After completing the HPC stream, students can appear for various competitive exams, including:

Civil Services and Administrative Exams

  • UPSC Civil Services Exam (IAS, IPS, IFS)
  • State Civil Services Exams

Law Entrance Exams

  • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test)
  • AILET (All India Law Entrance Test)
  • LSAT India (Law School Admission Test)

Psychology-related Exams

  • UGC NET (National Eligibility Test)
  • Entrance Exams for M.A./M.Sc. in Psychology

Social Work and Welfare Exams

  • CSW (Certified Social Worker) Exams
  • Entrance Exams for MSW (Master of Social Work)

Humanities and Social Sciences Exams

  • IISER Aptitude Test
  • Entrance Exams for Journalism and Mass Communication

Teaching and Education Exams

  • CTET (Central Teacher Eligibility Test)
  • TET (State Teacher Eligibility Test)

Public Administration and Policy Exams

  • Exams for Public Administration and Policy programs (e.g., TISS)

Other Exams

  • Hotel Management Entrance Exams (e.g., NCHMCT JEE)
  • Airline and Travel Management Exams (e.g., IATA)

Career Paths after Completing HPC

Students from the HPC stream can pursue a wide range of careers:

  • Teaching (Humanities, Civics, or Psychology)
  • Lawyer (after completing law studies)
  • Psychologist/Counselor
  • Civil Servant (IAS, IPS, IFS)
  • Social Worker (in NGOs, government welfare agencies)
  • Journalist/Media Professional
  • Public Administrator
  • Political Analyst
  • Human Resource (HR) Professional

Conclusion

The HPC stream offers students a strong foundation in social sciences, law, psychology, and public administration. By qualifying for relevant competitive exams, students can pursue a fulfilling career in various sectors such as government, law, social work, media, and education. The stream provides ample opportunities to contribute meaningfully to society and grow professionally in these fields.
