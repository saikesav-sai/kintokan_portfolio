Diploma in Textile Designing

Comprehensive training in textile patterns, fabric selection, and design techniques

About

A diploma in Textile Designing provides students with knowledge of fabric patterns, weaving techniques, dyeing methods, and digital textile design. It prepares individuals for careers in fashion, interior design, and textile manufacturing.

Key Components

  • Textile Materials and Fabrics: Understanding different types of fabrics, their properties, and applications.
  • Weaving and Knitting Techniques: Fundamentals of fabric construction and pattern creation.
  • Dyeing and Printing: Various textile coloring techniques, including block printing and digital printing.
  • Computer-Aided Textile Design (CATD): Use of software tools to create textile patterns and designs.
  • Fashion and Home Textile Trends: Study of market trends, sustainability, and innovative textile solutions.

Practical Training

  • Internships in textile mills, fashion houses, or design studios.
  • Hands-on experience with textile designing software and machinery.
  • Creating and showcasing textile collections through projects and exhibitions.
