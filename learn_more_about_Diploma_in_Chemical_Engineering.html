Diploma in Chemical Engineering

Comprehensive training in chemical processes and engineering principles

About

A diploma in Chemical Engineering equips students with the fundamental knowledge and technical skills required for careers in the chemical, pharmaceutical, and manufacturing industries. The program provides practical training in chemical processes, material science, and environmental engineering.

Key Components

  • Chemical Process Engineering: Understanding the fundamentals of chemical reactions, process control, and plant operations.
  • Material Science & Engineering: Learning about different materials, their properties, and applications in industry.
  • Thermodynamics & Fluid Mechanics: Studying heat transfer, energy systems, and fluid dynamics.
  • Environmental & Safety Engineering: Training in pollution control, waste management, and safety regulations.
  • Industrial Chemistry & Quality Control: Ensuring product quality and compliance with industry standards.

Practical Training

  • Laboratory experiments in chemical analysis and synthesis.
  • Training in industry-standard software like Aspen HYSYS and MATLAB.
  • Internships or projects with chemical plants and research labs.
  • Developing technical reports and safety protocols.

Career Opportunities

  • Quality Control Analyst
  • Environmental Engineer
  • Production Manager
