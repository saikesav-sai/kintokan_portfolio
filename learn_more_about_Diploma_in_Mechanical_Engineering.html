Diploma in Mechanical Engineering

Comprehensive training in mechanical systems, design, and manufacturing

About

A diploma in Mechanical Engineering provides students with essential knowledge in mechanical design, thermodynamics, material science, and manufacturing processes. The program prepares students for technical roles in various industries.

Key Components

  • Engineering Mechanics: Principles of forces, motion, and structural analysis.
  • Thermodynamics: Study of heat transfer, energy systems, and fluid mechanics.
  • Manufacturing Processes: Techniques such as machining, welding, and casting.
  • Mechanical Design: CAD modeling and product development.
  • Automobile Engineering: Fundamentals of vehicle design and maintenance.

Practical Training

  • Workshops on machine tools, CNC programming, and robotics.
  • Internships in automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries.
  • Project-based learning in design and fabrication.

Career Opportunities

  • Mechanical Engineer
  • Automobile Technician
  • Manufacturing Supervisor
  • CAD Designer
  • Quality Control Engineer
