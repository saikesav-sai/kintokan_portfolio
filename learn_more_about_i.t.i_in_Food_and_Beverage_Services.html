ITI in Food and Beverage Services

Comprehensive training in hospitality and food service management

About

The ITI in Food and Beverage Services provides students with essential skills and knowledge required in the hospitality industry. This program covers food preparation, service techniques, hygiene standards, and customer service management.

Key Components

  • Food Service Techniques: Table setting, order taking, and customer interaction.
  • Beverage Management: Understanding beverages, preparation, and responsible service.
  • Hygiene and Safety: Food safety protocols, sanitation, and personal hygiene.
  • Hospitality Management: Basics of hotel and restaurant operations.
  • Customer Relations: Effective communication and handling guest needs.

Practical Training

  • Internships in hotels, restaurants, and catering services.
  • Hands-on training in food handling, table service, and beverage preparation.
  • Case studies and simulations to enhance problem-solving skills in hospitality.

Career Opportunities

  • Food Service Supervisor
  • Restaurant Manager
  • Bartender
  • Catering Coordinator
  • Hotel Staff (Food & Beverage Department)

Further Studies

  • Diploma in Hotel Management or Culinary Arts.
  • Certifications in food safety, beverage management, and customer service.

Summary

The ITI in Food and Beverage Services equips students with the necessary skills to excel in the hospitality industry. With hands-on training and industry exposure, graduates can pursue careers in food service, catering, and hotel management.
